About

Who We Are

CFPA is the independent, member‑led association for Crumbl franchise partners. We organize owner priorities, collaborate with corporate on data‑driven improvements, and help members operate smarter—while educating franchisees on their rights under FTC rules.

How we work

  • Member‑Led. Transparent. Action‑Oriented.
  • Volunteer board, working committees, and monthly chapter calls.
  • Issue pipeline: intake → research → member vote → proposal → follow‑up.
  • Quarterly system updates and published priorities.
  • Professional AAFD chapter management and voting tools.

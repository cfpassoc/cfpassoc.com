Our goal is to have a unified voice and work together with Crumbl to improve operations and profitability. We are committed to understanding our rights as franchisees under the laws governed by the FTC.

The Crumbl Franchise Partner Association (CFPA) unites franchise partners to share solutions, strengthen profitability, and work constructively with corporate—backed by the resources of our AAFD chapter (joined Dec 2024).

About

Who We Are

CFPA is the independent, member‑led association for Crumbl franchise partners. We organize owner priorities, collaborate with corporate on data‑driven improvements, and help members operate smarter—while educating franchisees on their rights under FTC rules.

How we work

  • Member‑Led. Transparent. Action‑Oriented.
  • Volunteer board, working committees, and monthly chapter calls.
  • Issue pipeline: intake → research → member vote → proposal → follow‑up.
  • Quarterly Crumbl CEO meetings for updates and published priorities.
  • Professional AAFD chapter management and voting tools.
